Welcome to Fitneer

We all know that regular exercise is good for both your physical and mental well-being. However, studies have shown that on average people give up on exercising entirely in less than two weeks. Why is that? I get it, working out can be difficult, time consuming, results are only visible after a number of weeks and sometimes it's just not fun... Being on your own in the gym or while training outside is often not fun. Tracking progress, coming up with new exercises which are suited for you can be rather boring. Have you ever felt like you wanted to share your journey with other who could understand you, someone you can relate to or vice verse. We know that we're not the only ones struggling with getting leaner, fitter or stronger. However, that relatable person, or people can sometimes be difficult to find. Only if you could find an accountability partner, someone who is going through the same hardships and experiencing the same joys of working out. Someone whom you can give a push when they feel down and expect him or her to be there for you when you feel quitting.

If so, 'Fitneer, find your fitness peer' is the right place for you. We'll help you find people who train for the same reason as you, who have a similar schedules, who are of the same fitness level and most importantly whom you can talk to, share progress and advice with, meet and work out together.

Sign up with one click and find peers in a matter of seconds.
